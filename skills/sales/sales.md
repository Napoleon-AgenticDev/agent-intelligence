# Sales Agent Skill

## Purpose
Enable AI agents to execute sales tasks including prospect research, lead qualification, outreach, and pipeline management.

## Triggers
- User asks to research a company
- Lead qualification requested
- Outreach sequence needed
- Meeting preparation required
- Proposal generation needed

## Workflow

### Phase 1: Research
1. Gather company information (size, industry, website)
2. Identify key decision makers
3. Research recent news and activities
4. Understand pain points and needs
5. Map to ideal customer profile

### Phase 2: Qualification
1. Apply BANT or MEDDIC framework
2. Score lead on fit and intent
3. Identify champion and blockers
4. Determine timeline and budget

### Phase 3: Outreach
1. Personalize message to prospect
2. Choose channel (email, LinkedIn, phone)
3. Create compelling hook
4. Include clear call to action
5. Set follow-up sequence

### Phase 4: Conversion
1. Prepare discovery questions
2. Handle objections
3. Build value proposition
4. Close on next steps

## Common Tasks

### Prospect Research
- Company firmographics
- Decision maker identification
- News and trigger events
- Competitive landscape

### Lead Qualification
- BANT assessment (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)
- MEDDIC assessment (Metrics, Economic buyer, Decision criteria, Identity, Champion)
- ICP fit scoring

### Outreach
- Cold email sequences
- LinkedIn outreach
- Cold call scripts
- Video outreach

### Pipeline Management
- Deal tracking
- Activity logging
- Forecast updates
- Report generation

## Tools
- CRM systems (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive)
- Sales intelligence (ZoomInfo, Apollo)
- Email platforms
- LinkedIn
- Meeting schedulers (Cal.com, Calendly)

## Rules
- Always personalize outreach
- Never misrepresent capabilities
- Qualify before pursuing
- Document everything in CRM
- Follow up consistently
- Handle objections gracefully
